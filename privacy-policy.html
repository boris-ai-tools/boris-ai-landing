Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the following Chrome extensions published by Boris AI: WriteFlow AI, ClauseGuard, and ReplyPilot.

Data We Collect

Our extensions collect and process the following data:

  • Text you submit for processing: When you select text or paste content for AI analysis, that text is sent to our AI provider (OpenRouter) for processing. The text is NOT stored on our servers after processing is complete.
  • Usage statistics: We track the number of requests you make (daily/monthly counts) locally in your browser storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • API key: If you provide an API key, it is stored locally in your browser's encrypted storage (chrome.storage.sync). We never transmit your API key to any server other than OpenRouter.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT store the content you process
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do NOT sell any data to third parties
  • We do NOT collect analytics or telemetry

Third-Party Services

Our extensions use OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) as an AI API provider. When you process text, it is sent to OpenRouter's servers for AI inference. OpenRouter's privacy policy applies to that data transfer. We recommend reviewing OpenRouter's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All user preferences and usage data are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage APIs (chrome.storage.sync and chrome.storage.local). This data syncs across your Chrome instances if you're signed into Chrome, using Google's encrypted sync infrastructure.

Data Retention

Text submitted for processing is transmitted to the AI provider in real-time and is not retained after processing. Local usage statistics reset monthly (for scan-based limits) or daily (for request-based limits).

Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all local extension data by right-clicking the extension icon → "Manage Extension" → "Clear Data"
  • Uninstall the extension at any time to remove all associated data
  • Use the extension without providing any personal information

Children's Privacy

Our extensions are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
